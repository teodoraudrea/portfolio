## Overview
Supported the revamp of direct postgraduate application forms by gathering requirements and
organizing usability testing with internal stakeholders.

## Highlights
- Coordinated usability testing sessions and documented findings.
- Translated stakeholder feedback into actionable improvements for the development team.
- Ensured alignment with governance and user needs.

## Outcomes
Improved form usability and clarity, reducing friction in the application process.
