## Overview
A playful, easy-to-follow explanation of CSR, SSR, SSG, ISR, and DSG using culinary metaphors to clarify trade-offs and use cases.

## Key takeaways
- CSR favors interactivity but can delay first load and SEO benefits.
- SSR delivers fast, consistent pages at the cost of server complexity.
- SSG and ISR balance performance with freshness in different ways.
