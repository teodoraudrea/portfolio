## Overview
A reflective post on lifelong learning that explores how adults grow through curiosity, practice, and creativity—framed through the metaphor of nurturing a tomato plant.

## Key takeaways
- Lifelong learning thrives when it’s practical, relevant, and self-directed.
- Momentum matters: small “planting windows” help overcome hesitation.
- Balancing focused and diffuse thinking supports creativity and problem-solving.
