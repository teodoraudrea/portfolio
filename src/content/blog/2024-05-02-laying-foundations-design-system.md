## Overview
A summary of how foundational design-system principles—shared language, alignment, and iterative delivery—support long-term success in a university setting.

## Key takeaways
- Build on existing brand and component foundations.
- Establish clear terminology for components, patterns, templates, and pages.
- Focus on content needs and iterate toward an MVP.
